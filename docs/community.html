Get Involved

oVirt is a community driven virtualization project and people just like you are making it happen.

Getting Involved


The oVirt community is a group of multidisciplinary individuals who are contributing code, writing documentation, reporting bugs, contributing UX and design expertise, and engaging with the community.


Before getting started, we recommend that you:

  • Sign up for the users@ovirt.org mailing list and send us an email saying how you would like to contribute. Visit our mailing lists page for other oVirt mailing lists to sign up for.
  • For fluent, real-time communication, join us on IRC.
  • Please read our community etiquette guidelines. (Quick summary: Be nice!)

Translate


Want to help make oVirt's user interface and documentation available in multiple languages for users around the globe? Examples include Italian, Portuguese, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and more. To help out, sign up for the devel@ovirt.org mailing list.

Case Studies


Read our featured case studies and find out how a wide variety of organizations around the world are successfully deploying and benefiting from oVirt.

Design


Love design or UX? From concept through to implementation, help us to deliver a seamless user experience for oVirt users. To join us, sign up for the devel@ovirt.org mailing list.

Meet Our Infrstructure Team


The infrastructure team are a group of volunteers and professionals who manage the servers, build tools, and create new applications to help make oVIrt development a smoother process. We are located all over the globe and communicate primarily by the #ovirt channel on irc.OFTC.net and on the infra@ovirt.org mailing list.

Documentation


Good at technical communication? oVirt needs concise, reader-friendly, up-to-date installation and usage documentation. To help out, log in to our source repository.

Find and File Bugs


Help us to shake bugs out of oVirt and create a better user experience for all. You can report oVirt bugs on Red Hat bugzilla. If you do not have a Bugzilla account yet, you can create one here. Need to report a security issue? Please follow the security reporting procedure.
